Reflection

Thought Git was scary, turns out it's just a smart save button with receipts. Learned alot about proper work flow and how to think about when a commit is a good idea.
  1. After finishing a task or feature
  2. After fixing a bug or layout issue
  3. After setting up a new page or section
  4. Before switching to someting unrelated. (e.g., done with nav bar-> now working on image styling)
Commit rules of thumb
  • Don't over commit: No need to commit every 2 minutes
  • Don't Under commit: Don't make 25 changes and then commit. Hard to roll back or review
  • Think Checkpoints, not perfection: "Would I want to come back to here is something breaks?"
