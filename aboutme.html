About Me

Hey, I am Trevor Holmes—a husband, father of two, and a self-taught developer-in-training working through The Odin Project.

This site is my coding journal: part portfolio, part sandbox, part progress log. I use it to document what I am learning, build projects, and track my growth as I move from HTML and CSS into JavaScript and beyond.

Why am I doing this? Simple—I am tired of the ad-stuffed, data-hungry mess that makes up most of what is out there for kids today. I want to build better things—apps and tools that actually teach, inspire, or just don't suck the soul out of you. So here I am, learning the craft one line of code at a time. Thanks for stopping by.
